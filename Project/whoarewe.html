Who Are We?

Flipside is a vintage diner, providing good tunes, good times and good food. Flipside is the place to go for an experience like none other. We play non-stop throwback hits so you're transported as soon as you walk in. For disco fever and great deals, there's no place better!

We're bringing it back to the old school, so come on in and experience a blast to the past like you never seen it and great food to match it too. We have an old-timey booth layout and retro furnishings to add the perfect final touch. We serve diner style foods with modern twists.

Flipside Bar is located just upstairs of Flipside Restaurant. This is the perfect place to relax, catch up, and just have fun. Admission is strictly 18s and up. Visit and see for yourself if we're worth the hype!
